INSTRUCTIONS:
Click on Play as X/O to start a game.
X always moves first.
The player is always red, the opponent is always blue.
The game follows standard tic tac toe procedures.
Click on any of the 9 squares to place a marker on them.
You can cheat and play on taken squares, but the AI won't be happy if you do.
